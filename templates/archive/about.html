About Us

Welcome to our group project!

Our project investigates the relationship between donations to presidential campaigns and the individuals who make them. We analyzed nearly 10 million campaign-finance records, explored them geographically and demographically, and then used machine learning to build a model that predicts the political persuasion of new donors.
